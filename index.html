Commercial Drone Pilot

Our Remote Pilot, John, is commercially licensed through the FAA (Part 107), and maintains additional licensing through the FCC and TSA. He's partial to flying FPV (first-person-view) and Cinematic FPV, but has experience operating many different drone platforms. Conducting a safe and successful flight mission is our overall priority, which is why Ordnance FPV is fully compliant with Remote Identification. Our Visual Observer utilizes a laptop with Software-Defined Radio (SDR) for manned-aircraft tracking, alongside an on-screen display that shows what the pilot sees though his goggles for added awareness. We commonly use drones for: Large Aerial Images; Indoor Drone Flythoughs; Weddings & Events; Realty Listings; Photogrammetry Maps & 3D Models; Roof & Building Inspections; Custom Drone Content
