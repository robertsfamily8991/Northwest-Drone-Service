John Roberts | FPV Drone Pilot

Ordnance FPV makes capturing photo and video from the air unique and exciting. I have several types of unmanned aircraft systems registered with the FAA and each one provides a unique ability.

Traditional GPS and sensor-stabilized drones offer a modern solution for airborne inspections, property mapping, outdoor events and security patrols.

FPV or, first-person-view, drones offer a perspective that cannot be matched by stabilized camera drones, and can safely operate at a much lower speed and altitude than a manned-aircraft. These drones are much faster than traditional camera drones and are capable of exceeding the 100mph speed limit established by the FAA. FPV drones can be used to film high-speed race events, extreme sports and up-close and personal cinematic action.
